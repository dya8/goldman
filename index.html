Started 25 years ago by Shanavas Hassan, Sakthi Gold has grown into a trusted plywood and timber brand with a strong presence across India. Known for its high-quality local and imported plywood, Sakthi Gold is one of the region's top timber exporters—driven by quality and customer-first values.
